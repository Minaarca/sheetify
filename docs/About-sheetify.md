## Welcome to Shetify 🚀

**Shetify** is a powerful Shopify app designed to simplify store management, improve efficiency, and help merchants grow faster with smart automation tools.

Whether you're a small business owner or managing multiple Shopify stores, Shetify provides reliable, secure, and performance-focused solutions built specifically for Shopify merchants.

---

## 🌟 What is Shetify?

Shetify is a modern Shopify application that integrates directly with your store to provide enhanced functionality, automation, and optimization tools.

Our mission is simple:

> **Make Shopify management easier, smarter, and more efficient.**

## 🛠 How Shetify Works

1. Install Shetify from the Shopify App Store.
2. Connect your Shopify store securely via OAuth.
3. Configure app settings based on your needs.
4. Start optimizing your store instantly.

Shetify integrates directly with Shopify APIs to ensure smooth, reliable communication between your store and the app.
